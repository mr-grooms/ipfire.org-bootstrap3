IPFire is free of cost and does not generate any direct sort of income. It is funded by donations and although it is financially supported by its own community of users, its ambition is to compete with projects which are backed by large companies.

Financial ressources are essential for the IPFire project to pay for servers and equipment, finance the needs of the distribution and fund the work of the people behind it.

We encourage anyone who downloads and uses IPFire to contribute to the distribution: Please donate and encourage others to do so as well. It is very much appreciated.
