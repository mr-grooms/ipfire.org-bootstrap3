The wiki is maintained by community. Feel free to log on with your account and share your knowledge. This is the only way to get the wiki to the best source of information about IPFire.

FORUM The second place is the forum. If your question was not answered in the wiki you may probably want to ask it there.
 
BUGTRACKER

If you are sure (and only if) you found a bug in the IPFire system, you may file it against the developers.
This is a way to improve the quality of IPFire.

For historical reasons there are two bug tracking systems available. We use mantis for IPFire 2.x and earlier versions which is replaced in the development of version 3.x by redmine.

  • Development for IPFire 2.x
  • Development for IPFire 3.x
IRC

If you need immediate help there is the IRC channel where you can meet fellow IPFire users that may get their hands on your configuration and check it out together.
